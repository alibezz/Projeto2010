If negotiations are resumed: ramifications of failure

A Palestinian View
  • We shouldn't rush into negotiations
           by Ghassan Khatib

    We should not resume talks for the sake of having a process.

An Israeli View
  • Look at alternatives
          by Yossi Alpher

    Peace talks are best held between strong and cohesive governments that are in full control of their territory.


  • Failed negotiations could fatally undermine the Palestinian Authority
           by George Giacaman

    It should be obvious that unless there is also direct American pressure on the Israeli government there can be no agreement.

  • To negotiate or not to negotiate
           by Oded Eran

    The Palestinians and the US may take courage from the fact that every past round shortened the distance to the Israeli goalpost.
